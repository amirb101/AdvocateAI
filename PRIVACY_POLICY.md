# Privacy Policy for Advocate LLM

**Last Updated:** November 8, 2024

## Introduction

Advocate LLM ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### Data Processed Locally
- **Article Text**: When you use the extension, article text is extracted from web pages you visit. This text is processed locally in your browser before being sent to our backend API (if configured) or directly to LLM providers.

### Data Sent to Our Backend (if using backend)
- **Article Text**: Sent to our backend API for analysis
- **User ID**: A randomly generated identifier to track quota usage
- **Provider/Model Selection**: Which LLM provider and model you choose

### Data Stored Locally
- **API Keys** (if using direct API): Stored locally in Chrome sync storage, never sent to our servers
- **Settings**: Your provider/model preferences
- **Quota Information**: Daily usage count (stored locally or on our backend)

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identifying information (name, email, etc.)
- Browsing history
- Other websites you visit
- Any data from pages you don't actively analyze

## How We Use Your Information

- **Article Analysis**: To find polarising points and generate counterarguments
- **Quota Management**: To enforce usage limits (5 requests per day for free tier)
- **Service Improvement**: To improve our algorithms and fix bugs
- **Caching**: To cache analysis results and reduce API costs

## Data Storage and Retention

- **Article Text**: Cached temporarily (1 hour) to improve performance. Not stored permanently.
- **Request Logs**: Stored for analytics and debugging. No personal data included.
- **User Quota Data**: Stored to enforce rate limits. Reset daily.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following LLM providers (you choose which one):
- **OpenAI** (if selected): See [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- **DeepSeek** (if selected): See [DeepSeek Privacy Policy](https://platform.deepseek.com/privacy)
- **Anthropic** (if selected): See [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)

Article text is sent directly to your chosen provider. We do not control how they handle your data.

## Security

- API keys are stored securely on our backend server (never exposed to clients)
- All API communications use HTTPS
- We implement rate limiting to prevent abuse
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties

## Your Rights

- **Access**: You can view your quota status at any time
- **Deletion**: Clear extension storage to remove all local data
- **Opt-out**: Stop using the extension at any time

## Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.
